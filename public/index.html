About us

Apprise conf a run by a team of enthusiasts in the Norwegian tech industry. The conferences sole purpose is to host an event where people with the same interests can share knowledge, get to know each other and have fun!

The conference organization is a non-profit legal organization registered as Apprise. Any profit from the conference will be used for other related community events and next year's conference.
